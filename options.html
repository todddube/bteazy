About

Version: 1.0.0

Description: BTEazy makes downloading torrents effortless. Automatically converts magnet links, tracks download history, and features a modern dark UI.

How it works: This extension intercepts clicks on torrent links and downloads the .torrent file to your specified location. Magnet links are automatically converted to .torrent files. You can then manually add these files to your preferred BitTorrent client.
